What We Do

We Do Crazy Things

Learn

We don't focus on a particular technology, members bring their own expertise to share -- and a burning desire to learn..

Teach

The best way to understand a concept is to explain it to someone else. “While we teach, we learn,” through The Protégé Effect.

Grow

Our members come from different backgrounds, and as we learn new cool stuff, we share it. This way, everyone grows!

Repeat

There is always something to learn and mentor the next generation of engineers to encourage innovation.

Community

We connect and build a community of women of color in technology and close the gap in the digitial divide.

Engage

A lack of diversity is a social justice issue. We support members while they change their careers and change their life.

Help Us Change the World

You can help us improve the technology industry's diversity problem.
